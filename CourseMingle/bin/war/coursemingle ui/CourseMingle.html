Course Mingle

Welcome to Course Mingle!

Our application solves an irritating problem university students face term after term: finding out about what classes they have in common and any mutual breaks they may have with their friends. Currently, the solution to the above problem is an inefficient and time-consuming process. It goes something like this. A student that wants to share their course schedule takes a screenshot of their current course schedule and posts it on Facebook. We want to automate this process. With access and sufficient permission to ‘UBC Courses’ data we are able to create a much more seamless and efficient solution. A student simply pastes their schedule into our application and we do the rest. No more posting screenshots of your schedule to Facebook.
